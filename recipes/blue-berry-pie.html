Blueberry Pie

Crispy Blueberry pie with crumble dough

An easy-to-make but oh so delicious blueberry shortcrust pie. So go out into the woods and pick 1 liter of blueberries that you put in the bottom of a mold. Sprinkle classic shortcrust pastry over and bake in the oven. Serve your blueberry pie warm with smooth vanilla sauce, whipped cream or ice cream.

Ingredients

    Filling

  • 1 liter blueberries
  • 1 dl sugar (just over)
  • 2 tbsp potato flour

    • Shortbread

  • 150g butter
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 3 dl wheat flour

    • For serving

  • Custard sauce
  • Whipped cream
  • Ice cream/li>

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 200Â°C.
  2. Filling: Rinse and clean the berries. Mix them with sugar and potato flour.
  3. Shortcrust pastry: Melt the butter to make shortcrust pastry. Stir in the sugar and flour. Stir until it forms a dough. Let cool slightly.
  4. Line the bottom of a pie dish with the blueberries. Crumble the dough over it. Bake the pie for 15-20 minutes in the oven.
  5. To serve: Serve with Marsana sauce, whipped cream or ice cream.
